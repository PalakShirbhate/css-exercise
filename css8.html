Vegetable Masala Maggi Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Maggie Masala
  • cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Peas

Instruction to make maggie

  1. Heat pot and add butter
  2. add finely chopped vegetables
  3. cook the veggies for 2 min.
  4. add maggie masala
  5. now, add 500ml water and bring water to boil
  6. add cake of maggie and cook for 2min.
  7. place a cheese slice on maggie
  8. Serve hot and enjoy
